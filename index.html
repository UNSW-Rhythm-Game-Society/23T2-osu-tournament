Difficulty: 

The tournament will be available in two difficulty brackets

  • HARD [3.6 - 5.0⭐️]
  • EXPERT [5.0 - 6.6⭐️].

Map Pool:

The Structure of the Map Pool is as follows:

Number of MapsBracket
6No Mod
6Hidden
6HardRock
6Double Time
6Force Mod
1Tie Breaker

Total Number of Maps: 31

Additional Rules:

  • Force Mod: This bracket is played with ForceMod activated i.e every player must pick 1 or more mods from either Hidden or Hardrock or Flashlight.
  • Tie Breaker is played with ForceMod conditions but it is not necessary for players to enable mods.

Maps:

Maps are chosen with the following factors taken into consideration:

  • Maps suggested by applicants in the application form
  • Maps suggested by Volunteers and Tournament Staff
  • Popular Maps at the time of the tournament
  • Popular / New Anime songs

Team Structure:

The team structure for both brackets is as follows:

Team Size6 ≤ size < 12
Active Players4
Bench2 ≤ bench < 8

Additional Rules:

  • Teams are allowed to switch players between active and bench during the breaks provided between each song.
  • If a player is absent on the day and the team size does not have a minimum of 4 players then the team is disqualified.
    • If there are more than 2 teams participating then the < 4 players team will be added to the bench of either remaining valid teams.
  • If a team exceeds 12 players then it is to be divided into 2 seperate teams and the tournament structure changes.
  • Each Team must elect 1 leader to roll and message the staff on which map is to be picked or banned.
    • In order to reduce chances of confusion between staff and players during the match.

Score:

  • player's score will be calculated using score V2.
  • team’s wins will be evaluated using the Team VS mode in a multiplayer room.
  • Players will also have to use the NoFail mod during the course of every match
    • Clarification: This does not affect the score in V2

Picks and Bans:

  • The first map is picked by the team that wins the warmup game at the start of the tournament.
    • Winners can decide whether they want to pick or ban.
  • Teams Leaders will run a !roll 100 between maps, AFTER the first map, whichever team scores higher bans 1 from the bracket, and the other team leader picks the next map.
  • Each team will be allowed to ban 1 map per game (not including Tiebreaker).
  • Long break will be held midway through the game for a duration of 15 minutes.
  • Midway is defined as
  • Teams are allowed 2 minutes to pick/ban the next map, AND 2 more minutes to (optionally) switch active with bench players.

Clarifications:

  • Incase an active player disconnects between matches they will be allowed 10 minutes to rejoin.
    • If there are no bench players the game will end and the team will be disqualified if they are not able to join back within 10 minutes.
    • If the team has bench players the game will go on with a bench player put in an active player position.
  • If a player disconnects during a match and there is a valid reason, both teams will have to play the map again within 10 minutes of waiting for the player
    • if the player is unable to rejoin within 10 minutes a bench player will be chosen to play in their place.
    • if there are no bench players, there will be a total wait time of 15 minutes instead
      • if the player is still unable to join the match will end and the team will be disqualified.
  • If on the tournament day a team is unable to gather at least 4 active players the match will be rescheduled to a later date.
  • Teams will be allowed waiting time of up to 10 minutes before they start of the game to gather players and plan.
  • If any incident occurs outside the defined cases above, STAFF will be allowed to reach a decision on the matter based on their judgment of the situation at the time.
    • This decision will be considered final.

Summary of changes

  • HARD [3.6 - 5.0⭐️]
    • Increased lower limit from 2.6 → 3.6⭐️
    • Increased upper limit from 4.6 → 5.0⭐
    • Reason: Maps that were chosen didn’t really go below 3.6 stars previously so the lower limit was redundant.
    • 5 is so funny and cool 😎
  • EXPERT [5.0 - 6.6⭐️].
    • Lowered upper limit from 7.3 → 6.6⭐️
    • Reason: To make maps more S-able, as it is a better look for the competitions if scores range between A to S rather than C to S.
    • 5 is so funny and cool 😎
  • Maps
    • New total map pool size of 31 to increase available choice so the match does not get boring.
    • Defined multiple methods of how maps will be picked
    • introduced the role of volunteer and tournament staff
    • Reason: So that everyone is aware of how the map pool is decided this time, and can raise objections and opinions on the map pool depending on these criteria.
  • Teams
    • Defined the role of Team Leader
      • Reason: it was not in the rules last time but still implemented anyways.
    • Set a minimum team size of 6
      • Reason: so that there are at least 2 bench players.
    • Also made the rules around team sizes more defined in this version
      • Reason: helps avoid confusion.
  • Picks and Bans
    • picks and bans per game instead of once at the start and once midway
    • Added number limit for !roll between maps
    • Defined Midway between game
    • Re-phrased old rules.
  • Lots of changes to clarifications.
    • Made it more defined and removed some redundant cases.
